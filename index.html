Aimy's got you covered

Aimy believes that to overcome the feeling of always being rushed and to start making progress, you just need to follow 5 simple principles.

These principles are derived from existing research and Aimy’s own experiences.

1. Make up your mind

2. Specify your target

3. Commit yourself

4. Cut it into pieces

5. Start doing

1

Make up your mind

By choosing consciously

  • List all your dreams
  • Prioritize your dreams
  • Select the 3 most vital
  • Park all others

2

Specify your target

By filling in the details

  • Specify your active dreams
  • Know why you want it
  • Acknowledge the obstacles
  • Set a realistic deadline

3

Commit yourself

And involve others

  • Capture your specified dream
  • Set weekly action moments
  • Share it with others
  • Ask someone as a guard

4

Cut it into pieces

To make it feasible

  • Specify the big milestones
  • Set deadlines for each milestone
  • Formulate your next action
  • Visualize yourself completing this action

5

Start doing

And keep moving

  • Take the first step
  • Build a routine (by working on the
    realization of a dream at fixed times)
  • Visualize your progress
  • Celebrate small wins

Beauty and brains

Aimy gives you focus, helps you organize and keeps you motivated. And she’s pretty too.

Organize your dreams

Simply add any dream, enter when you’d like to realize it, and specify the main obstacle. Based on these preferences, Aimy ranks all your dreams and suggests which dream is most vital.

Focus on what’s essential

Activate your most vital dream; choose a category, create milestones, and set weekly action moments. Now, just start chasing your dream with full dedication.

Stay motivated

The only thing left is to just make it happen. Aimy’s dashboard is designed to let you do just that. It shows your progress and tells you what to do next to achieve your upcoming milestone.
